Process

How Labelyze works

01
Upload Label
Photo, PDF, or paste text directly. Tesseract OCR extracts the text automatically from images.
02
AI Analysis
Sentence Transformers NLP model checks meaning — not just keywords. Understands context the way a reviewer would.
03
Schedule Check
Drug identified from 100+ compound database. Schedule H, H1, X, OTC, or banned status confirmed instantly.
04
Risk Score
Compliance score calculated. Risk level: LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH. Clear release decision generated.
05
Deviation Report
PDF report with findings, rule citations, corrective action box, and QA sign-off section — ready to attach to batch records.
Capabilities

Built for QA teams.
Not enterprise budgets.

⚡
AI Semantic Matching
Uses Microsoft's all-MiniLM-L6-v2 model to understand label text beyond keywords. Detects "Valid until" as an expiry date even without the word "expiry".
🛡
Banned Drug Detection
Identifies drugs banned in India under Section 26A of the Drugs & Cosmetics Act — with plain-English regulatory explanations per compound.
📋
QA Deviation Reports
Generates professional PDF reports with compliance scores, risk levels, critical findings, and sign-off sections — formatted for batch records.
🔬
Drug Schedule Database
100+ compound database covering Schedule H, H1, X, OTC classifications. Know which schedule your drug belongs to before release.
🌐
Dual Market Mode
Indian market (CDSCO Schedule M) and International export (FDA/ICH guidelines) — in one tool. Built for India's export-oriented manufacturers.
💊
FDA DailyMed Integration
Live connection to the US National Library of Medicine's drug label database for FDA reference label comparison in export mode.
